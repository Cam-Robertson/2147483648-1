How to play 1024 Game?

How to play: Watch video and try to win on 1024 game! Use your arrow keys or W S D A to move the tiles. When two tiles with the same number touch, they merge into one tile with same number they summed to one ! 1+1 -> 2, 2+2 -> 4, 4+4 -> 8 etc. 512+512=1024
