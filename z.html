How to play: Use your arrow keys to move the tiles. When two tiles with the same value touch, they merge into one!

When two positive or negative tiles collide with themselves, they form a tile with twice its value.

When two positive and negative tiles collide with one another, they form a lettered tile.

When two tiles with the same letter touch, they merge into one tile with letter incremented by 1! AA -> B, BB -> C, CC -> D etc.

Created by Gabriele Cirulli. Based on 1024 by Veewo Studio and conceptually similar to Threes by Asher Vollmer.
