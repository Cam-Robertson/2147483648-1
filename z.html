How to play: Use your arrow keys to move the tiles. When two tiles with the same value touch, they merge into one!

When two tiles with the same value collide, they merge into one. Two neutral zero tiles will merge together to form one 0 tile, and would just occupy a space on the grid without merging.

When two tiles with the same absolute value collide, they merge into one to form a Unicode character on the logarithmic scale, starting with A (Unicode character #65, or 0041.). i.e. 2 and -2 (2¹) 3 and -3 (1.5×2¹) will merge to form A, regardless of the base number.

When two Unicode characters of the same kind touch they merge into a tile with the next Unicode character! For a challenge, try to beat the game by going to 10FFFF!
